Services

Permit Acquisition

End-to-end permitting for seismic surveys, pipelines, transmission lines, and other energy projects. We coordinate with agencies, surface owners, mineral rights holders, and all relevant stakeholders to keep projects on schedule.

Title & Land Research

Ownership verification, deed review, and mineral rights tracing performed directly at county and municipal offices. We deliver clear reports for project planning and risk management.

Field Oversight & Closeout

On-site agents during active operations for monitoring, damage documentation, and communication. Projects close only after restoration is verified and all items are resolved with the landowner.
