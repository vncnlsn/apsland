How It Works

From first
research to
final closeout.

Every project follows the same sequence. Here's where APS fits at each step — and why it matters.

Full process breakdown
01

Project Scoping & Title Research

Before anyone knocks on a door, we map the parcels, trace ownership, and flag complications. The goal is a permit schedule that holds up — not one that surprises everyone mid-project.

02

Landowner Contact & Permit Negotiation

Our agents make personal contact with every landowner — by phone and in person. We explain the project plainly, answer real questions, negotiate access terms, and document every conversation.

03

Field Oversight & Damage Management

APS agents are on-site during operations to make sure crews stay inside the agreed access terms. If something goes wrong, we're already there — not responding after the landowner calls your office.

04

Restoration Verification & Closeout

We verify the property is restored to the agreed condition, settle any remaining claims, and close out the project completely. A clean closeout protects your ability to go back.
