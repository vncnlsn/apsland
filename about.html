Our Story

Four decades of
doing it right.

APS was built on the premise that this industry runs on relationships — with agencies, with landowners, with the communities where our clients work. That's been true since 1980, and it's still true today.

Who We Are

Not a staffing firm. Not a law firm. Just the best people on the ground.

Allstate Permit Services started in 1980 with a simple idea: energy and infrastructure projects need experienced, trustworthy people to handle the land and permitting side of the work.

Over the past 46 years, we've grown into a nationwide firm with deep regional expertise, a team of seasoned professionals, and a reputation built on one thing: showing up and getting it done. Our clients come back to us because they know what they're getting — experience, accountability, and people who treat landowners the way they'd want to be treated themselves.

We're not the biggest firm in the space. We're the most reliable one. And for most of our clients, that's the only thing that matters.

Our History

Built over decades,
not assembled overnight.

  • 1980

    Founded

    Allstate Permit Services is established, beginning with permit coordination for oil and gas exploration across the Mid-Continent region.

  • 1990s

    Expanded Nationwide

    Growing demand from seismic and pipeline clients extends APS's operations to the Rocky Mountain, Gulf Coast, and Appalachian regions.

  • 2000s

    Courthouse Research Division

    APS formalizes its title and ownership research practice, bringing courthouse expertise in-house to serve complex infrastructure projects.

  • 2010s

    Renewable Energy Projects

    APS expands into wind and solar right-of-way and permitting work, applying decades of landowner relations experience to the growing renewable sector.

  • Today

    Nationwide, Trusted, Still Growing

    APS continues to serve the country's largest energy and infrastructure companies with the same field-first approach that's defined us since day one.
